Yes, cat-breeding can be ethical, but only if it is performed by a licensed breeder for research purposes. An ethical breeder will have the sole intention of improving the breed rather than profiting from it. Illegal breeding takes place when pure-bred/pedigree cats are forced to breed in very dire situations, which results in a plethora of health problems. Illegal cat breeding is done to get the desired physical appearance of a pedigree cat, and not for the betterment of the cat or the breed.
